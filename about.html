About Us

Oblix Pharma is a forward-thinking global pharmaceutical company dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare access through reliable, efficient, and compliant supply chain solutions. Our comprehensive services include procurement, storage, distribution, and specialized cold chain logistics for life-saving medicines, vaccines, and rare disease treatments. We are driven by a passion for excellence and innovation, ensuring timely delivery and complete regulatory adherence from enquiry to last-mile delivery. Our expertise in Named Patient Supply, Clinical Trial Supply, and Cold Chain Management makes us a trusted partner in global healthcare.

What We Do

We deliver comprehensive pharmaceutical procurement and distribution services, specializing in critical-care and specialty medicines. Our offerings include named patient supply to clinical trial logistics and emergency hospital support, our solutions are backed by robust regulatory expertise and quality assurance. Through certified partners and global networks, we source even rare drugs with speed, safety, and precision.

Our Mission

To improve global access to healthcare by delivering high-quality pharmaceutical products through reliable, responsive, and ethical supply practices. We aim to empower healthcare providers with timely access to trusted medicines — transforming patient care, one shipment at a time.

Our Vision

To become a globally admired and future-ready pharmaceutical partner known for unlocking access to critical and innovative therapies across borders. We envision building a smarter, more agile healthcare supply ecosystem — one that thrives on transparency, technology, and trust — Our vision is to be a one-stop solution for all healthcare needs.
